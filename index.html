How LeadHive works

  1. Search any category in Google Maps (e.g. "salons in Bengaluru").
  2. Open LeadHive and click Auto-Extract Leads.
  3. LeadHive scrolls the results, collects links and opens each place to read details.
  4. Data is saved locally and you download a CSV.

Frequently asked questions

Why are some phone numbers missing?

Some businesses do not share phone numbers publicly; LeadHive extracts only what is visible on the place page.

Does LeadHive upload my data?

No. All processing runs locally in your browser; no extracted data is transmitted to our servers.
